The Point Childcare - Photoshoot

My work, Dream Config had me working with a childcare centre in an Auckland suburb to create content for their website we were developing. This was a big challenge for me as not only was this on the client's budget but also I had to keep in mind that the identy of the children were not overpowering the shots. We wanted to produce emotional photographs that emphasised the childcare's values. Overall, I was very happy with the outcome and the photos actually lead on to another client asking for photos of their childcare centre.
